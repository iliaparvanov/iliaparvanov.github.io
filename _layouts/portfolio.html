What can I do for you?

The core of my freelancing services is developing Wordpress websites. Wordpress is the most popular CMS (and will continue to be for the foreseeable future) due to several factors: it is very SEO-friendly (in other words, Wordpress websites usually rank well on Google and other search engines), it is customizable and, above all, it is easy to use even for non-techies (after the initial set-up and development). So, if you are interested in building any kind of website where you want to be able to edit the content (eg a blog, a portfolio or even a store), Wordpress is most likely the right choice.
I also offer other services for customers who desire something simpler or would like to avoid Wordpress for another reason. I design and develop static websites (ie sites without editable content) and simple blogs which are easier to manage than a full-fledged Wordpress blog.
Have something in mind which was not mentioned above? Contact me and we can work out the details!
